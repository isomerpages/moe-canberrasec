<p>Canberra Secondary School celebrated its annual Awards Day on 6 March
under the theme "Leading with Kindness; Learning for Life". The ceremony
recognised Canberrans for both academic excellence and co-curricular achievements,
reinforcing the school's commitment to nurturing well-rounded individuals.</p>
<p>A standout moment was the valedictorian address by Adam Jeremy Didonato,
who spoke movingly about personal growth through Values-in-Action experiences
and expressed heartfelt gratitude to his teachers and the school community.</p>
<p>The school also honoured its valued community partners, Friends of Canberra,
for their generous support in enriching Canberrans' learning journeys.
The event was a celebration of character, compassion, and lifelong learning.</p>
