<p><strong>Lunar New Year Celebration cum Mother Tongue Language Fortnight 2026</strong>
</p>
<p>In 2026, our school proudly celebrated the Lunar New Year Celebration
cum Mother Tongue Language Fortnight, a vibrant event that brought together
culture, language, and community. Aligned with our school's mission of
"Growing Minds, Glowing Hearts" and our commitment to nurturing Future-Ready
Canberrans, this programme provided students with an authentic and immersive
platform to appreciate, experience, and actively use their Mother Tongue
in meaningful cultural contexts.</p>
<p><strong>Mother Tongue Language Fortnight</strong>
</p>
<p>During the fortnight, students participated in engaging activities designed
to spark joy in learning and strengthen cultural pride. These experiences
embodied our school motto of "Challenge, Conquer &amp; Contribute" as students
challenged themselves to try new activities, conquered language barriers,
and contributed to our vibrant school community.</p>
<p>Students explored traditional art forms including Chinese calligraphy,
fan painting, lantern making, and festive crafts at Cultural Booths, whilst
enjoying interactive games such as Chinese yoyo, iron-hoop rolling, pitch-the-pot,
and spinning top. Chinese Language Games including puzzles, charades, and
word challenges strengthened language proficiency whilst making learning
enjoyable. Booths introducing traditional Chinese medicine knowledge and
Chinese etiquette helped students understand cultural practices in daily
life.</p>
<p>The fortnight also featured exciting in-house competitions: Hard Pen Calligraphy
Competition, Chinese Character Typing Competition, Picture Description
Competition, Passage Recital Competition, and the Lunar New Year Comic
Drawing Activity.</p>
<p><strong>Lunar New Year Celebration</strong>
</p>
<p>The festivities culminated on 13 February when the school community came
together to usher in the Lunar New Year with joy and enthusiasm. Students
participated in the Lunar New Year Classroom Decoration Competition, transforming
spaces with vibrant festive ornaments and creative displays.</p>
<p>The celebration concert featured outstanding performances by our Performing
Arts CCAs: Concert Band, Guzheng Ensemble, Dance Club, Guitar Ensemble,
and Choir. External performances brought traditional culture to life, including
festive drums and stilt walking, the captivating Sichuan Opera mask-changing
act, and an energetic Lion Dance performance. The God of Fortune's special
appearance brought blessings and well wishes to all, whilst our in-house
Lunar New Year MTV showcased student and staff creativity.</p>
<p>Through these festive performances, interactive cultural activities, and
friendly competitions, students experienced the richness of Chinese culture
whilst strengthening unity across the school. This celebration exemplified
our commitment to growing minds through cultural learning and glowing hearts
through community connection.</p>
<p>We extend our heartfelt appreciation to all Parent Support Group members
for their invaluable support and contributions, making this celebration
a resounding success in nurturing confident, compassionate, and culturally
grounded Future-Ready Canberrans.</p>
