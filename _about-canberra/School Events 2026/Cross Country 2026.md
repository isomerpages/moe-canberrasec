<p>Canberra Secondary School's Annual Cross Country on 10 April 2026 brought
students together for a 3.3km run through Admiralty Park, starting from
Republic Polytechnic.</p>
<p>Whether racing for a top-ten finish or enjoying a leisurely jog through
the park, every Canberran took part in a morning that was as much about
community as competition. Road marshals, first aid responders, and uniformed
group volunteers — from NCC, NPCC, NCDCC, Boys' Brigade, and Girls' Brigade
— ensured the event ran safely and smoothly.</p>
<p>The day ended with cheers for the top finishers and a well-deserved lunch,
leaving students with full stomachs and lasting memories.</p>
