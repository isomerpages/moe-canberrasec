<p>As part of the Cultural Programme Exposure Scheme (CPES), Canberra Secondary
School's Chinese Language Unit organised two enriching learning journeys
this year.</p>
<p>Secondary One and Two Higher Chinese students attended "Lao Jiu: The Musical"
(老九) at the Drama Centre, a vibrant production adapted from Kuo Pao Kun's
beloved play. Secondary Two G2 and G3 Chinese students experienced "Here
Comes Crosstalk 2026" (相声来了2026) at the Singapore Chinese Cultural Centre,
enjoying a lively and interactive performance.</p>
<p>Both learning journeys deepened Canberrans' connection to Chinese language,
culture, and heritage — bringing the classroom to life beyond its four
walls.</p>
