<p>Canberra Secondary School's Hari Raya Concert 2026 was a joyful celebration
of culture, community, and student talent. The concert featured a kompang
performance by Secondary One and Three Canberrans, an energetic silat display,
a generational skit by Wira Budaya, and a heartfelt guitar and vocal rendition
of "Suasana Hari Raya" by Canberrans from Secondary Three and Four.</p>
<p>The event was a wonderful opportunity for Canberrans to explore the traditions
of Hari Raya while developing their confidence and performance skills.
The concert concluded with a lively dance and sing-along that had the whole
school in festive spirits.</p>
<p>For many performers, it was their first time on stage — an experience
that taught them courage, teamwork, and the power of community.</p>
