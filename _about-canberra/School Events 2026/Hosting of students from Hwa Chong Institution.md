<p>In March 2026, Canberra Secondary School hosted twenty students from Hwa
Chong Institution (HCI) for a week-long local student exchange programme.
Sixteen Canberrans served as hosts, welcoming their HCI counterparts to
lessons and co-curricular activities.</p>
<p>The exchange fostered cross-cultural understanding, strong interpersonal
bonds, and meaningful conversations between students from different educational
backgrounds. It concluded with a farewell session and group photographs
— mementos of friendships built across school boundaries.</p>
<p>This programme reflects Canberra's commitment to developing Canberrans
who are open-minded, resilient, and ready to engage with the wider world.</p>
