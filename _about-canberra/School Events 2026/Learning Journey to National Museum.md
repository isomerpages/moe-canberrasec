<p>Canberra Secondary School's Secondary Two cohort visited the National
Museum of Singapore as part of their History curriculum, guided by specially
trained museum educators.</p>
<p>Engaging with artefacts and exhibits across the museum's galleries, Canberrans
explored Singapore's history and heritage in an immersive setting. The
experience brought their classroom learning to life and deepened their
sense of Singaporean identity.</p>
<p>Learning journeys like this are an integral part of how Canberra Secondary
connects students to the world beyond the classroom.</p>
