<p>Canberra Secondary School's Model United Nations (MUN) programme gives
Canberrans a platform to explore international relations, diplomacy, and
debate at a competitive level.</p>
<p>Trained by experienced delegates from the United Global MUN group — including
students from Raffles Institution, Crescent Girls' School, Catholic High
School, and St Nicholas Girls' School — our Canberrans have represented
the school at VCA MUN 2025, UG MUN 2025, and RV MUN 2026.</p>
<p>Through MUN, Canberrans develop critical thinking, public speaking skills,
and the ability to engage with complex global issues. It is a programme
that prepares young Canberrans not just for school, but for the world.</p>
