<p>Four Canberrans — Florah Esther Prince, Brayden Khoo Wee Kiat, Aathithya
City Babu, and Luzaina Khalil Baig — represented Canberra Secondary School
at the NUS Geography Challenge 2026 on 28 February, themed "City for Tomorrow:
Shaping Our Liveable Future."</p>
<p>The team competed in both the Individual and Team Components of the Preliminary
Round, as well as the Creative Component, which involved fieldwork on Singapore's
liveability. Brayden Khoo Wee Kiat was awarded Silver and Aathithya City
Babu Bronze — strong results that reflect their passion for the subject.</p>
<p>Team leader Luzaina Khalil Baig described the experience as "one of the
most intellectually stretching" of her secondary school journey.</p>
