<p>On 27 February 2026, the Canberra Secondary School Parents' Support Group
(PSG) organised a Lunar New Year lohei celebration, bringing together Canberrans,
families, and staff for an evening of warmth and community spirit.</p>
<p>The event featured the beloved tradition of tossing yusheng, accompanied
by laughter, hearty wishes, and ice-breaker games that got everyone mingling.
It was a joyful reminder that Canberra Secondary School is, at its heart,
a close-knit community of Canberrans.</p>
<p>Our deepest thanks to the PSG for their dedication and warmth in making
the evening so special.</p>
