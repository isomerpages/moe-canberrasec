<p>Canberra Secondary School's Scrabble club made a remarkable impact in
its debut competitive season. Formed in late 2025 by thirteen Canberrans
under the guidance of Mr Koh Tze Hou and Coach Ben, the team trained diligently
every Tuesday and quickly developed into formidable competitors.</p>
<p>At the 2025 Super Zonals held at Mind Cafe, Team 4 clinched third place
against established competitors including MSHS and MSS. One of their players
was also named the competition's best player.</p>
<p>Their story is one of determination, teamwork, and the joy of mastering
something new from the ground up.</p>
