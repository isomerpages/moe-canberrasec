<p>Canberra Secondary School closed Semester 1 on a high note with the Sports
Challenge 2026 — a spirited competition that brought together Canberrans
across all levels in the true spirit of sportsmanship and community.</p>
<p>Throughout the challenge, students competed with determination and heart,
demonstrating the values that define a Canberran: resilience in the face
of adversity, camaraderie in victory and defeat, and integrity in every
contest. Congratulations to all level champions and participants who gave
their best on the field.</p>
<p>The event was a meaningful reminder that excellence at Canberra Secondary
extends beyond the classroom. Whether competing, cheering from the sidelines,
or supporting behind the scenes, every Canberran played a part in making
the Sports Challenge a celebration of school spirit and collective pride.</p>
<p>We are grateful for the support of our students, staff, and organisers
who worked together to make the event a success — and for the sunshine
that graced us just in time.</p>
<p>As we head into the June holidays, we look forward to returning in Semester
2 stronger, more united, and ready to Challenge, Conquer, and Contribute.</p>
