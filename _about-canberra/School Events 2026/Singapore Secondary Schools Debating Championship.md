<p>Canberra Secondary School's English debate team made an impressive debut
at the Singapore Secondary Schools Debating Championship, advancing all
the way to the Quarter Finals — a remarkable achievement for a team of
Canberrans who started from scratch.</p>
<p>Made up of seven Canberrans, most with no prior debate experience, the
team spent long afternoons training together, building arguments, and supporting
one another through every round. Their journey taught them that debate
is not just about speaking — it is a collective effort built on teamwork
and trust.</p>
<p>The team extends their heartfelt thanks to their coaches Mr Koh, Ms Diyanah,
Ms Goh, Ms Wong, and senior Don for their guidance and belief in the team.</p>
