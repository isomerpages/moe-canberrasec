<p>On 13 May, Canberra Secondary School held its Student Leaders' Investiture,
formally recognising newly appointed Canberran student leaders across all
roles and responsibilities.</p>
<p>This year's ceremony was distinctive in that it was largely planned and
executed by students themselves. From coordinating across teams to managing
tight timelines, Canberran organisers demonstrated exactly the qualities
that the investiture seeks to celebrate: leadership, adaptability, and
service.</p>
<p>Student Council President Kor Jia Xuan Garnet Faith from 3 Diligence reflected
that the experience reminded her that "every contribution, no matter how
small, matters." A message we hope every Canberra leader carries forward.</p>
