<p>Art in Canberra Secondary is dedicated to fostering creativity, critical
thinking, and self-expression through a dynamic and comprehensive art curriculum.
Our programmes are designed to nurture students' artistic abilities while
developing their appreciation of art in its various forms and mediums.</p>
<p></p>
<h2>Lower Secondary Curriculum</h2>
<p>At the Lower Secondary level, students embark on an exciting journey of
exploration across diverse artistic media through:</p>
<p><strong>Core Skills Development:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Drawing fundamentals and techniques</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Color theory and application</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Basic painting techniques (watercolor, acrylic)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Introduction to sculpture and 3D forms</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Digital art foundations</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Learning Outcomes:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Understanding of art elements and principles</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Basic proficiency in various art media</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Development of observational skills</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Introduction to art appreciation and history</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Foundation in creative thinking and expression</p>
</li>
</ul>

<h2>Upper Secondary Curriculum</h2>
<p>Our Upper Secondary Art Programme is tailored to meet the requirements
of different academic streams while nurturing each student's artistic potential.</p>
<p><strong>Express Stream (O-Level Art)</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Advanced drawing and painting techniques</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Study of art movements and artists</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Portfolio development</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Art criticism and analysis</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Preparation for O-Level coursework and examination</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Normal Academic (N-Level Art)</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Intermediate art techniques</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Focused skill development</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Simplified art history and appreciation</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Modified portfolio requirements</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Preparation for N-Level assessment</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Normal Technical Stream</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Practical art applications</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Basic design principles</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Hands-on project work</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Focus on visual communication</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Portfolio building for further education</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>
