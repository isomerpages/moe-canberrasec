<p>Science Department @ Canberra Secondary School – An extraordinary world
where curiosity flourishes and innovation thrives!</p>
<p></p>
<p>Our thoughtfully crafted curriculum nurtures a love for inquiry-based
learning, inspiring your child to explore, question, and discover. We place
emphasis not only on scientific knowledge but also on cultivating values
of gratitude for our natural world, respect for diverse perspectives, and
compassion for the global community – fundamental principles that prepare
students for the challenges of tomorrow.</p>

and a deep appreciation for scientific exploration. Our devoted educators

a genuine enthusiasm for scientific investigation and developing skills
crucial for our ever-changing world.</p>
