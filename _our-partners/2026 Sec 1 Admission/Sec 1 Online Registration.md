<p>Steps for completing the Sec 1 Registration:</p>
<p>1. Download the Parents Gateway mobile application from the Apple App
Store or from Google Play.</p>
<p>2. Login to Parents Gateway. Canberra Secondary School will be automatically
reflected on Parents Gateway once you have received your child’s school
posting.</p>
<p>3. Look for the notification on the Parents Gateway app.</p>
<p>4. Click on the “Acknowledge” button at the bottom of the notification
to complete the Sec 1 registration process.</p>
