<p>Eligible students from Posting Groups 1 and 2 can offer English Language,
Mother Tongue Languages, Mathematics and Science at a more demanding level
(MDL) based on their PSLE Achievement Level (AL) for each of these subjects.</p>
<p>Eligible students will receive a notification and the MDL Offer Letter
via Parents Gateway.</p>
<p>Please use the Online Acceptance Form in the Parents Gateway notification
to indicate your child’s/ward’s acceptance of the MDL offer by 5 pm on
Tue, 23 Dec 2025.</p>
