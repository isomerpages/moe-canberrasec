<p><strong>Overseas Service-Learning Programme 2025: Cambodia</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Siem Reap, Cambodia: 26 - 30 May 202</strong>
</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/WhatsApp_Image_2025_05_27_at_1_07_54_PM__1_.jpg">

<p>Selected Secondary 3 Canberrans embarked on an unforgettable service journey
to Siem Reap, Cambodia, from 26 to 30 May 2025 as part of our Overseas
Service-Learning Programme (OSL). This meaningful experience marked a significant
milestone in their service-learning journey, encouraging our students to
mature and become more thoughtful about the importance of giving back to
communities.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/WhatsApp_Image_2025_05_28_at_9_44_20_AM.jpg">
</div>
<p>OSL encourages our Canberrans to critically engage with key topics such
as community engagement, cultural diversity, sustainability, and active
citizenship. Divided into THREE teams, each team conducted lessons for
beneficiaries in various subjects, including English, Mathematics &amp;
Science, Arts &amp; Crafts, Music, and Physical Education. The programme
fostered empathy and strengthened students' commitment to serving the greater
public good while broadening their global perspectives.</p>
