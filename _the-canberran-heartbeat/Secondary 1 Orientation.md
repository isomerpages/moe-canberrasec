<h4><strong>Secondary 1 Orientation Programme</strong></h4>
<p>Welcome to Canberra Secondary School! Our Secondary 1 Orientation Programme
is designed to help new students transition smoothly into secondary school
life. Through a series of engaging activities, students will bond with
their new classmates, learn important skills, and explore our school's
facilities.</p>
<p><strong>Objectives of the Secondary 1 Orientation Programme</strong>
</p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Formally Welcome Secondary One Students</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>We warmly welcome all new students to the Canberra Secondary School community.</p>
<ol start="2" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Establish a Support System:</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>We strive to create a supportive environment where Secondary One students
can form strong connections with their peers and teachers, ensuring a smooth
transition into secondary school life.</p>
<ol start="3" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Foster Bonding with Classmates and Schoolmates:</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>We provide various team-building activities for students to create long-lasting
friendships and develop a sense of camaraderie within their new classes
and with schoolmates.</p>
<ol start="4" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Familiarise Students with School Culture:</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>We introduce the school's Vision, Mission, Values, and Goals through our
Student Leaders, helping students to understand and embrace the Canberra
Secondary School learning culture and values.</p>
