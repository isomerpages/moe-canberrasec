<p>Canberra Secondary School's Robotics team has achieved a remarkable milestone
by winning the Create Award at the Singapore VEX V5RC National Championship
2025. This prestigious recognition celebrates exceptional creativity in
engineering design, showcasing our students' exceptional innovation and
engineering capabilities.</p>
<p>The Create Award specifically recognizes teams that demonstrate outstanding
creativity in their engineering design process. Our Canberrans developed
unique strategies that maximized scoring efficiency while implementing
innovative technical solutions that set them apart from other competitors.</p>
