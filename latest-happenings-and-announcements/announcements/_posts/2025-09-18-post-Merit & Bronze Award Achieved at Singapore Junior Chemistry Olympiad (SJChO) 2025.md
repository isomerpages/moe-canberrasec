<p>A group of students from 4 Empathy participated in Round 1 of the <strong>Singapore Junior Chemistry Olympiad (SJChO) 2025</strong> organised
by Singapore National Institute of Chemistry (SNIC). SJChO provides a platform
for all upper secondary students to challenge themselves in their Chemistry
knowledge and skills. It also seeks to promote the excitement in learning
and doing Chemistry as well as to identify and develop Chemistry talents.</p>
<p>Two of our students, <strong>Ramesh Hrithik </strong>and <strong>Zhang Duoduo</strong>,
from <strong>4 Empathy </strong>were shortlisted for Round 2 of the competition. <strong>Hrithik</strong> achieved
the <strong>Bronze Award</strong> while <strong>Duoduo</strong> received the <strong>Merit Award</strong>.
Hrithik was invited to the Award Ceremony held at MOE Building to receive
his Bronze Award.</p>
<p>Congratulations to Duoduo and Hrithik!</p>
